# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers on LLM Output Diversity

1. **Generative Monoculture in Large Language Models** (2407.02209_generative_monoculture.pdf)
   - Authors: Fan Wu, Emily Black, Varun Chandrasekaran
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2407.02209
   - Why relevant: Defines generative monoculture, shows LLMs narrow output diversity. RLHF identified as key driver. Code at https://github.com/GeMoLLM/GeMo

2. **We're Different, We're the Same: Creative Homogeneity Across LLMs** (2501.19361_creative_homogeneity_across_llms.pdf)
   - Authors: Emily Wenger, Yoed Kenett
   - Year: 2025
   - arXiv: 2501.19361
   - Why relevant: Shows creative homogeneity exists across different LLM families, not just within one model. Key finding: LLM responses are far less variable than human responses.

3. **Magic, Madness, Heaven, Sin: LLM Output Diversity** (2604.01504_llm_output_diversity.pdf)
   - Authors: Harnoor Dhingra
   - Year: 2026
   - arXiv: 2604.01504
   - Why relevant: Unified framework for LLM output variation across epistemic/interactional/societal/safety contexts.

4. **A Comprehensive Analysis of LLM Outputs: Similarity, Diversity, and Bias** (2505.09056_llm_outputs_similarity_diversity_bias.pdf)
   - Authors: Multiple
   - Year: 2025
   - arXiv: 2505.09056
   - Why relevant: Large-scale analysis (3M texts, 12 LLMs) of output similarity patterns.

## Creativity Evaluation

5. **CreativityPrism: Holistic Evaluation Framework** (2510.20091_creativity_prism.pdf)
   - Authors: Multiple
   - Year: 2025
   - arXiv: 2510.20091
   - Why relevant: Decomposes creativity into quality, novelty, and diversity dimensions. 17 LLMs evaluated.

6. **LiveIdeaBench: Evaluating LLMs' Scientific Creativity** (2412.17596_liveideabench.pdf)
   - Authors: Multiple
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2412.17596
   - Why relevant: Benchmark for divergent thinking with 1,180 keywords across 22 domains.

7. **Divergent Creativity in Humans and LLMs** (2405.13012_divergent_creativity_humans_llms.pdf)
   - Authors: Multiple
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2405.13012
   - Why relevant: LLMs surpass average humans on DAT but not highly creative humans.

8. **Benchmarking Language Model Creativity: Code Generation** (2407.09007_benchmarking_lm_creativity.pdf)
   - Authors: Multiple
   - Year: 2024
   - arXiv: 2407.09007
   - Why relevant: Framework with Denial Prompting and NeoGauge metric for creative code generation.

## Novelty Measurement

9. **LLM Generation Novelty Through Semantic Similarity** (2510.27313_llm_novelty_semantic_similarity.pdf)
   - Authors: Davydov et al.
   - Year: 2025
   - arXiv: 2510.27313
   - Why relevant: Semantic novelty framework at pretraining scale. Released 20TB of corpus chunks.

10. **Multi-Novelty: Improve Diversity via Multi-Views Brainstorming** (2502.12700_multi_novelty.pdf)
    - Authors: Multiple
    - Year: 2025
    - arXiv: 2502.12700
    - Why relevant: Inference-time method to improve output novelty and diversity.

## Bias and Convergence

11. **Human Creativity in the Age of LLMs** (2410.03703_human_creativity_age_llms.pdf)
    - Authors: Multiple
    - Year: 2024
    - arXiv: 2410.03703
    - Why relevant: Randomized experiments on how LLMs affect human divergent/convergent thinking.

12. **Evaluating the Evaluation of Diversity in NLG** (2004.02990_evaluating_diversity_nlg.pdf)
    - Authors: Guy Tevet, Jonathan Berant
    - Year: 2020
    - arXiv: 2004.02990
    - Why relevant: Framework for evaluating diversity metrics. Shows decoding parameters mostly affect form, not meaning.

13. **LLMs as Recommender Systems: Popularity Bias** (llm_recommender_popularity_bias.pdf)
    - Authors: Lichtenberg, Buchholz, Schwöbel (Amazon)
    - Year: 2024
    - Why relevant: Studies popularity bias in LLM recommendations. Proposes principled popularity bias metric.

14. **Examining and Addressing Barriers to Diversity in LLM-Generated Ideas** (2602.20408_barriers_diversity_llm_ideas.pdf)
    - Authors: Multiple
    - Year: 2026
    - arXiv: 2602.20408
    - Why relevant: Identifies barriers to diverse idea generation and proposes solutions.
